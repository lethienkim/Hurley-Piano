About

HURLEY PIANO

Hurley Piano develops education methodologies for easy learning to help parents teach their kids music. Expertise focuses on kids with special needs as well as neuro-typical kids, such as: ADHD, Asperger Syndrome, Austism, Down Syndrome, Dyscalculia, Dyselexia, Sensory Processing Disorder.

Richard Hurley has developed a highly regarded Black Keys Exercises designed to introduce the world of the keyboard to the most challenging of special need students. Based in Austin Texas, a city that is a hub of special needs innovation, Hurley Piano has drawn upon the expertise of the city’s leading special needs researchers to test and challenge this methodology. Sign up for free introductory videos and blogs, follow the video based conversation around the challenges facing our special needs kiddos today.


My Mission


The Piano serves as a powerful educational tool for neurodiverse children. Its structured yet creative nature aids in cognitive development, improving focus, memory, and problem-solving skills. For neurodiverse kids, it offers a multisensory experience, enhancing sensory processing and coordination. Moreover, music fosters emotional expression and regulation, crucial for those with neurodivergent conditions like autism or ADHD. Through music education, these children find avenues for self-expression, confidence- building, and social interaction, promoting holistic development and inclusivity.
